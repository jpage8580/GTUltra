### 22. The Master Channel
a. To sync to the correct playing position, a channel needs to be selected as the “Master”. Syncing will then take place, based on this channel. The Master
    channel is defined by the cursor position. Either:
    i. The channel number within the pattern editor when editing patterns
    ii. The channel number within the order list
b. The Master channel is always highlighted in the OrderList view as a yellow arrow (in this example, channel 2 is the Master Channel)

    ![image](../images/manual/master-channel-img-1.png)

c. This then takes into consideration the different pattern lengths, pattern repeat commands, channel-specific tempo settings within the song.
    (*If ALL patterns were the same length and there were no repeat commands
    in the order list or channel-specific tempo changes, life would have been far
    simpler!)
